# Revenue, Marketing ROI & Leakage Analysis (Power BI)

## Business Problem
Understand true revenue growth, marketing efficiency, and operational leakage
across platforms, channels, and product categories.

## Key Questions Answered
- How much revenue is real vs price-driven?
- Which platforms deliver efficient growth?
- Where is revenue leaking (discounts vs returns)?
- Which categories and channels are risky?

## Data Model
- Sales fact (orders, revenue, returns)
- Product & platform mappings
- Marketing spend (platform-level)
- Price change events with cumulative impact

## Key Metrics
- Net Revenue
- Adjusted Revenue (price-adjusted)
- Marketing ROI
- Leakage %
- True Growth (volume-driven)
